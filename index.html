Training Programs

Structured pathways to swimming excellence

Foundation Swimming Program

Perfect for new swimmers or those returning to the pool. Focus on building confidence and proper technique.

8-12 weeks
2-3 sessions/week
Max 6 students
  • Water safety and confidence building
  • Basic stroke technique (freestyle, backstroke)
  • Breathing coordination
  • Pool etiquette and safety

Stroke Development Program

For swimmers with basic skills ready to refine technique and build endurance across all four strokes.

12-16 weeks
3-4 sessions/week
Max 4 students
  • All four competitive strokes
  • Turns and starts technique
  • Endurance building
  • Individual medley introduction

Performance Enhancement Program

High-level training for competitive swimmers focused on speed, efficiency, and race preparation.

16-20 weeks
4-5 sessions/week
Max 3 students
  • Advanced stroke refinement
  • Speed and power development
  • Race strategy and pacing
  • Mental performance training

Elite Athlete Program

Exclusive training for national and international level swimmers preparing for major competitions.

Year-round
6-8 sessions/week
1-on-1 only
  • Biomechanical analysis
  • Periodized training plans
  • Competition preparation
  • Recovery and injury prevention
